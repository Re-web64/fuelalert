Privacy Policy

Thank you for using the Fuel Alert Page. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data and how we handle it.

The purpose of the privacy page

Our Privacy Policy aims to inform you about the types of information we collect, the methods of data collection, the purposes of data collection, how we use and share your data, your rights as a user, data security measures, our use of cookies and tracking technologies, and our compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

Types of Information collected

When you use the Fuel Alert page, we may collect the following types of information:

- Personal Information: Such as your name, email address, and contact details when you voluntarily provide them to us.

- Location Information: If you grant us permission, we may collect your geolocation information to provide you with fuel alerts based on your current location.

- Usage Data: Information about your interactions with the Fuel Alert page, including the features you use, the pages you visit, and the actions you take. This may include log data, IP address, browser type, device information, and timestamps.

Data Collection Method

We collect the information mentioned above through the following methods:

- Information You Provide: You may provide us with your personal information when you sign up for fuel alerts or contact us through the website.

- Automatic Data Collection: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect usage data automatically. These technologies help us analyze user behavior, improve our services, and customize your experience.

Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and use your information for the following purposes:

- To Provide Fuel Alerts: We use your location information to deliver relevant fuel alerts and notifications based on your preferences.

- To Improve and Personalize the Service: Your usage data helps us understand how users interact with the Fuel Alert page, enabling us to enhance and customize the service to meet your needs.

- To Communicate with You: We may use your contact details to respond to your inquiries, provide support, and send you important updates or notifications.

- To Ensure Legal Compliance: We may process your data to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal obligations.

Data Usage and Sharing

We respect your privacy and will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your data in the following circumstances:

- Service Providers: We may engage trusted third-party service providers who assist us in delivering the Fuel Alert service. These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and solely for the purposes specified by us.

- Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law, governmental request, or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

- Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or any form of sale or transfer of some or all of our assets, your data may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will ensure that the recipient of your data commits to privacy practices consistent with this Privacy Policy.

User Rights

As a user of the Fuel Alert page, you have certain rights regarding your personal information. These rights may include the right to access, correct, or delete your data, as well as the right to object to or restrict certain data processing activities. To exercise your rights or make inquiries regarding your data, please contact us using the information provided in this Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. While we strive to secure your data, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on the Fuel Alert page, analyze user behavior, and deliver personalized content. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please refer to our Cookie Policy for more details.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our data practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates. The updated Privacy Policy will be effective upon posting on the Fuel Alert page.
